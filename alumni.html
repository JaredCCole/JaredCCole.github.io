Alumni Activities

HOMECOMING

Every year, we are always excited to welcome back our alumni for homecoming weekend. It's a great time to hear stories across generations, and reminise with old friends.

ANNUAL SLAM GOLF TOURNAMENT

The Slam Golf Tournament is an annual get together of alumni at the Toftrees Golf Resort. These Phi Who men enjoy their early tee time and a fun day through 18-holes. Following a long day hitting the greens, we have our annual pig roast, where the undergraduate brothers and alumni brothers gather for delicious food and good times.

ALUMNI DINNER

The alumni dinner is a fun event that brings the brotherhood together across generations. We're always excited to welcome our alumni brothers back home for a delicious dinner and good drinks!

Getting in Touch

Our journey as Phi Kaps began in college, but our legacy continues forever as Phi Who men.

Our alumni are the most valued and cherished assets of our brotherhood. As our brothers embark into the next stage of their lives, it is our hope that they stay connected and informed about updates within our chapter.
