WHO We Are

We are a diverse group of like-minded individuals that want to make the most out of our college experience. Phi Who breeds better men to be effective leaders who passionately serve society, Fraternity, and God.


We take pride in the work hard, play hard mentality, as we are not just a social fraternity; we are also a professional organization. Through hard work and education, our brothers become outstanding men that are well-equipped for future endeavors. We reward resourcefulness, drive, and quality effort in order to foster an environment with countless leadership and social opportunities!
