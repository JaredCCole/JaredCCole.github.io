Phi WHO?

“
We are a diverse group of like-minded individuals, who want to make the most of our college experience. Phi Who helps boys become men, with the mission to breed leaders, who passionately serve society, Fraternity, and God.


We take pride in the work hard, play hard, mentality. We are both a social fraternity, and a professional organization. The brotherhood provides opportunity for leadership experience, community involvement, and development of personal skills. Our brothers are taught hard work, and good character. The best part? They graduate with memories and friendships, that last a lifetime.


Phi who is a brotherhood, where you can grow into a man of virtue.


Give, expecting nothing thereof.

- Phi Who Brothers”

PHILANTHROPY

For the past 5 years, we have broken our THON record every year. This year we raised $110,289.40, smashing our record from the previous year of $40,000.00.

COMMUNITY SERVICE

At Phi Who, we believe community service is an essential ingredient for breeding true gentlemen. We strongly encourage and incentivise our brothers to become better citizens and neighbors through mentoring, community service projects, and campus leadership.

SCHOLARSHIP

Phi Who proudly puts scholarship first. We never forget the real reason we are here: to get a quality education. For that reason, we are proud to have a 3.36 GPA, the 3rd highest GPA in the IFC!
